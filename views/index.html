API Craft

Our standards are simple - we are a community of individual API practitioners who see building APIs as a craft.

Join the discussion on Google Groups.

  • We represent our own opinions, and not that of employers or anyone else.
  • The forum is open to thoughtful posts from prospective employers who want great API thinkers and doers.
  • The forum is not open to posts from vendors who are promoting their products or spamming recruiters.

keep it real

We want honest discussions between people about their craft, not pitches. Most of us work for companies - we get it. Many of us will also be working for a different company 'round this time next year. There are 1000 other events you can go to where you can hear other people's sales pitches. Let's keep apicraftconf about the people, ideas, and technology - just like the apicraft forum.

open spaces

We're holding this as an Open Spaces conference because we believe the ethos of Open Spaces fits very well with the premise of the api craft forum.

Open Space Technology is an approach for hosting events focused on a specific and important purpose or task — but beginning without any formal agenda, beyond the overall purpose or theme. The meeting's participants create the agenda for themselves. All participants are given both the right and the responsibility to maximize their own learning and contribution. When you lose interest and get bored in a breakout session, or accomplish and share all that you can, the charge is to move on...use your two feet, go to a different breakout session.

Adapted from Open Space Technology on Wikipedia.
