We’ve had inspiring discussions online. We’ve met at regional meetups. We’ve gathered at other people’s conferences. Is it time the global API Craft community gets together for our own party conference? Absolutely!

We are still working out the details, but here’s the plan so far:

  • Date: Tuesday July 30th & Wednesday 31st, 2013
  • Place: To Be Determined
  • Who: API Craftsmen and Craftswomen
  • Spots: 150-200
  • Format: Open Space
  • Rules: No Corporate Logos, No Pitches, Represent Thyself Not Thycompany
  • Cost: ~$300 (Expenses / Number of Attendees)
  • Pre-Party: Yes
  • Intra-Party: Yes
  • Post-Party: Yes

We want honest discussions between people about their craft, not pitches. Most of us work for companies - we get it. Let's keep apicraftconf about the people, ideas, and technology - just like the apicraft forum.
